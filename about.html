About Pragati Book Store

Your trusted partner in the world of books since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Pragati Book Store began as a small independent bookstore with a simple mission: to connect readers with books that inspire, educate, and entertain. What started as a single storefront has grown into a beloved community hub and online destination for book lovers across the country.

Over the years, we've remained committed to our core values of exceptional customer service, carefully curated selections, and fostering a love of reading in our community. Our knowledgeable staff are passionate readers who love helping customers discover their next favorite book.

Our Mission

To provide a welcoming space where readers of all ages and interests can discover quality books, engage with literature, and become part of a vibrant reading community. We believe in the transformative power of books and strive to make reading accessible to everyone.
