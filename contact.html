Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery options?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days), expedited shipping (2-3 business days), and in-store pickup for local customers.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

What is your return policy?

You can return books within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, provided they are in original condition.

Do you buy used books?

Yes, we accept used books in good condition for store credit. Please bring them to our physical location for evaluation.
